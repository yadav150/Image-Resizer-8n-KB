Online Image Resizer (KB)
Upload an image and enter target size.

About This Tool

This online image resizer allows you to quickly reduce the size of your images to a specific target in KB without losing significant quality. It supports popular formats like JPEG, PNG, and GIF. The resizing happens locally in your browser, ensuring privacy and security of your images.

How to Use

Simply upload an image, enter the desired target size in KB, and click "Resize Image". Once resized, the button changes to "Reset" so you can clear the form and start over. A download link and preview of the resized image are provided immediately.

Note

Reducing images to very low KB may affect quality. Adjust target size accordingly and use the preview to check the result before downloading.
