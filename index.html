About This Tool

This online image resizer is designed to help you quickly and efficiently reduce the file size of your images to a specific target in kilobytes (KB). It supports various image formats including JPEG, PNG, and GIF, allowing you to optimize your images for web usage, email attachments, or storage without compromising on quality. The tool is ideal for users who need smaller images for faster upload times, limited storage, or better performance on websites and applications.

The resizing process happens entirely within your browser, which means your images remain private and secure. No data is uploaded to any external server, ensuring complete confidentiality. By using advanced compression techniques, the tool maintains the visual quality of your images even after reducing the file size. You can adjust the target size easily, and the tool automatically calculates the best compression level to match your requirement.

Once the resizing is complete, you receive a download link to save the optimized image immediately. Each image can be resized multiple times, and the process is fast and simple, making it convenient for both personal and professional use. This tool eliminates the need for complicated software, providing a lightweight and accessible solution for image optimization in just a few clicks.
